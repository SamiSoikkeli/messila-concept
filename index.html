The largest ski resort in Southern Finland, just an hour from the Helsinki metropolitan area. 10 slopes, 4 restaurants and accommodation for up to more than 200 people.

Old traditions are well alive in Messilä today. The main building in the court yard is the beautiful Manor House , built in the beginning of 1900, now transformed into a pleasant restaurant and meeting place. The biggest skicenter in Southern Finland has totally 10 slopes.

In the same yard You can find ski rental, ski school, out door activity company and on summer time golf course. Messilä is surrounded by woody ridges of Salpausselkä, and the scene to the lake is clear and gorgeous.
